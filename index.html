Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026 · Effective immediately

This Privacy Policy describes how LuneMart ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our comprehensive e-commerce marketplace—offering everything from groceries and electronics to fashion and home essentials. By using LuneMart, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and profile photo when you create an account.
  • Order Information: Purchase history, order details, delivery addresses, and payment method details (we do not store full card numbers).
  • Location Information: Your approximate location (e.g., Kidwai Nagar 208011) to show nearby supplier shops and relevant content.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and app usage data.
  • Communication Data: Messages you send to our customer support team.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, products viewed, search queries, and interaction patterns within the app.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your LuneMart account
  • To process orders and facilitate payments
  • To show you relevant products, brands, and supplier shops near your location
  • To send order confirmations, shipping updates, and delivery notifications
  • To provide customer support and resolve disputes
  • To personalize your shopping experience and show targeted recommendations
  • To send promotional offers, flash sale alerts, and discount coupons (with your consent)
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud
  • To analyze and improve our app's performance and user experience

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Suppliers: Your name, delivery address, and order details are shared with the relevant verified supplier to fulfill your order.
  • Service Providers: We use trusted third-party services for payment processing, delivery tracking, analytics, and cloud storage.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business Transfers: In case of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred to the acquiring entity.
We never share your personal data with advertisers for direct marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure servers with regular security audits
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal data
  • Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Payment data is handled via PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways

While we take every precaution, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and keep your account credentials confidential.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal information through your profile settings.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time via app settings.
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing where we rely on consent as the legal basis.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@lunemart.in

Cookies & Tracking

Our app uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. These include:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the app to function properly (login sessions, cart data).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our app to improve performance.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings like location and language preferences.

You can manage cookie preferences in your device settings, though disabling essential cookies may affect app functionality.

Children's Privacy

LuneMart is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete it. Parents who believe their child has provided information to us should contact us immediately.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request account deletion at any time. Following deletion, we may retain anonymized data for analytics purposes. Certain information may be retained longer as required by law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we make significant changes, we will notify you via the app or email. Continued use of LuneMart after policy changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Refund & Cancellation

Last updated: April 3, 2026 · We value your satisfaction above all else

At LuneMart, we strive to ensure every purchase leads to a smile. This policy explains how we handle returns, cancellations, and refunds for our wide range of products—from perishables and groceries to electronics and home goods.

Order Cancellation

  • For most categories (Fashion, Electronics), you can cancel an order within 1 hour of placement or before it's shipped, whichever is first.
  • Grocery & Daily Essentials: These orders cannot be cancelled once the supplier has begun processing the fulfillment due to the perishable nature of the items.
  • If an order is cancelled by the supplier due to stock issues, a full refund will be processed immediately.

Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund or replacement in the following scenarios:

  • The product received is physically damaged or defective.
  • The product received is significantly different from its description in the app.
  • The product arrived past the guaranteed delivery window.
  • Missing items from a multi-item order (pro-rata refund).

Category-Specific Rules

  • Groceries/Perishables: Refunds or replacements are only processed if reported within 6 hours of delivery with photographic evidence of damage/spoilage.
  • Electronics: Many electronics carry a 'Replacement Only' guarantee. Refunds are only issued if a replacement unit is unavailable.
  • Fashion: Standard 7-day return policy applies (unused, tags intact).
  • Hygiene Products: Personal care, masks, and undergarments are non-returnable unless the seal is broken or defective.

Refund Timeline

Once a refund is approved, the funds are credited back via the original payment method:

  • UPI/Wallets: 1-2 business days.
  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-7 business days depending on your bank's processing time.
  • LuneMart Credits: Instant (if applicable).

Cookie & Tracking Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026 · Helping us personalize your journey

LuneMart uses cookies and similar tracking technologies (like local storage and web beacons) to help optimize your shopping experience—remembering your location for groceries, tracking your cart across sessions, and personalizing product recommendations.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help our app remember who you are and what your preferences are. This technology is standard for almost all professional e-commerce applications.

Essential Cookies

These are necessary for the app to function properly. We cannot provide our services without them. They include:

  • Authentication: Keeping you logged into your account.
  • Shopping Cart: Saving items you've added to your cart so they don't disappear.
  • Security: Protecting your data and preventing fraudulent activities.

Experience & Analytics

These help us learn how you use LuneMart so we can make the app better:

  • Product Recommendations: Remembering what you've viewed to suggest things you'll love.
  • Location Data: Automatically showing you verified suppliers and groceries available in your specific area (e.g., Kanpur).
  • App Performance: Identifying technical issues before they affect your experience.

Managing Your Data

You can control tracking settings through your browser or device settings. However, please note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from making purchases or staying logged in.
